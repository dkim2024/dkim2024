To Make a 16" Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 360g flour
  • 234g water
  • 2g active dry yeast
  • 9g salt
  • 9g sugar
  • High Quality Tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • Low moisture Mozzarella Cheese (preferably full fat but part skim will work)
  • optional: = "https://www.amazon.com/Scratch-Premium-Dough-Conditioner-Improver/dp/B08NWBWMHB/ref=sr_1_1_sspa?keywords=dough+conditioner&qid=1636939693&sr=8-1-spons&psc=1&spLa=ZW5jcnlwdGVkUXVhbGlmaWVyPUExNzlITlowOU1FOE9OJmVuY3J5cHRlZElkPUEwNTI4NDM2WDZINUJLTUk5VTFCJmVuY3J5cHRlZEFkSWQ9QTA3ODIyNjQzTzNEN1dXQ0VGS0xZJndpZGdldE5hbWU9c3BfYXRmJmFjdGlvbj1jbGlja1JlZGlyZWN0JmRvTm90TG9nQ2xpY2s9dHJ1ZQ==" target = "blank"> king arthur's dough enhancer
  • optional: parmesan cheese (pre grated is fine)

Recipe Instructions

  1. Optionally bloom the yeast to check if it is alive by setting aside some of the water and sugar, and adding the yeast into the warm water and sugar solution.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a food processor, stand mixer, or just mix by hands until all ingredients are incorporated and the dough is slightly tacky. Depending on what equipment you use the kneading time will vary.
  3. Let the dough rise for at least 2 hours at room temp, or do an overnight cold ferment in the fridge. This will get you better flavor and allow for a bit more gluten development.
  4. For the sauce, simply puree or hand crush some high quality tomatoes with a bit of olive oil, salt, and sugar to taste.
  5. Once it's ready to bake, stretch the dough out onto a peel and dress with the sauce. Then optionally sprinkle or grate on a thin layer of parmesan cheese. Finish by topping with a thin layer of low moisture full fat mozzarella
  6. Bake in a preheated pizza oven if possible. If not, preheat a pizza stone and your oven to the highest temperature possible for at least half an hour, then slide the pizza out onto the pizza stone. If the cheese doesn't brown quickly enough, use the broiler.
  7. Enjoy your pizza!
